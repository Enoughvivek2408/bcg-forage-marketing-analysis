# Key Insights

## Project Objective
Analyze marketing campaign performance to identify the best campaign and marketing channel for acquiring new customers.

## Analysis Performed
- Created Pivot Tables in Microsoft Excel.
- Compared campaign performance.
- Compared marketing channel performance.
- Analyzed campaign and channel combinations using Pivot Charts.

## Key Findings
- Campaign B generated higher new customer sales than Campaign A.
- Email was the highest-performing marketing channel.
- The Campaign B + Email combination achieved the strongest sales performance.

## Recommendation
Increase investment in the Campaign B and Email combination to maximize new customer acquisition and improve marketing ROI.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- PowerPoint
